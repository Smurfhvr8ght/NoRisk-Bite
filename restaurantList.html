Galapung Bakehouse & café

Address: Jl. Siaga Raya No.19, Pejaten Bar., Ps. Minggu, Kota Jakarta Selatan, Daerah Khusus Ibukota Jakarta 12510, Indonesia

Description: Known for being fully gluten-free (they use cassava or rice flour instead of wheat) and offering pastries/desserts that avoid common allergens like gluten.

Valitas Gluten-free Bakery & Coffee

Address: RT.1/RW.9, West Cakudng, Cakung, East Jakarta City, Jakarta 13920, Indonesia

Description: All products advertised as gluten-free; they also offer vegetarian/vegan/dairy-free menus.

Kemankan Bakes

Address: Santa Modern Market 1st Floor Food Court Area - KFC 10, Jl. Cipaku I No.125, RT.5/RW.4, Petogogan, Kec. Kby. Baru, Kota Jakarta Selatan, Daerah Khusus Ibukota Jakarta 12170, Indonesia

Description: A small bakery whose owner reportedly developed recipes to avoid gluten (since the owner has gluten allergy themselves) — homemade cakes and sweets without wheat flour.
